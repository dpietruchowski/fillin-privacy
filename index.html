Privacy Policy — FillIn

Last updated: 27 June 2026

FillIn ("the app") is a language-learning application. This policy explains what data the app processes and how. FillIn is designed to keep your data on your device. The developer does not operate any server and does not collect, store, or receive any of your personal data.

1. Data stored on your device

The following is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to the developer:

  • Your learning content: expressions, translations, example sentences, and study progress (stored in a local SQLite database).
  • App settings and preferences.
  • Your AI provider API key, if you choose to use the AI features. It is kept in the device's secure storage (Android Keystore) and is used only to authenticate your requests to the AI provider you selected.

The app has no accounts, no login, and no registration. This data is removed when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

2. AI features and third-party providers

FillIn includes optional AI-assisted features (chat/agent and vocabulary generation). When — and only when — you use these features, the text you enter (for example words, phrases, or messages) is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you have configured, using your own API key, in order to generate a response. The developer is not an intermediary and does not see or retain this content.

The AI provider can be one of:

  • OpenAI — Privacy Policy
  • OpenRouter — Privacy Policy

Your use of these features is subject to the privacy policy and terms of the provider you choose. If you do not use the AI features, no data leaves your device for this purpose.

3. Text-to-speech

If you use the pronunciation (text-to-speech) feature, the relevant text is passed to your device's built-in text-to-speech engine, provided by your operating system. This processing is handled by your device/OS and is governed by your device manufacturer's and operating system's policies.

4. Data the developer collects

None. FillIn does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services, and the developer has no backend that receives your data.

5. Data sharing

The developer does not sell or share your data with anyone. The only external data transfer is the one described in section 2, which goes directly from your device to the AI provider you select, at your initiative.

6. Children

FillIn is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

Your API key is stored using the device's secure storage. Data transmitted to AI providers is sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
